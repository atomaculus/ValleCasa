CASA DEL VALLE

A culinary paradise with views to the Nevado de Cachi

NUESTRA CASA

Casa del valle is a project that brings together the different areas of the region to provide those who visit with a unique cultural experience through gastronomy and local handcrafts

Immersed in the unique Valle CALCHAQUI, Casa del Valle conveys the sensibility and susteinability of the local comunity, managing natural resources, earth and biodiversity with care.


SABORES DEL VALLE

Casa del Valle's unique restaurant allows guests to sample the region's local cuisine, passed down from the indigenous cultures of the north. Dishes are cooked with ethically sourced local products, as well as the restaurant's own farm and cellar where sustainability is the number one priority. The gastronomic proposal enriches the visitor´s palates an teaches travelers some of the local customs that remain in the region while enjoying the magnificient views of the Nevado de Cachi.

ARTESANIAS DEL VALLE

Casa del Valle also has its own gift shop wich shows an exquisitelly curated selection of handcraft objects. Aiming to aid local manufacturers, Casa del Valle offers fair trades wich provides work and resources for the locals.
